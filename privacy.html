Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Who We Are & Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Rizz Bot ("we", "us") handles information for: (a) the Rizz Bot iOS application (the "App"), and (b) this marketing website (the "Website"). The App and Website have different data practices, which are described separately below.

App (iOS) — What We Collect

  • Dating conversation data: text input and screenshots you provide for AI response generation. This data is processed temporarily to generate responses and is not stored permanently. Your conversations and personal messages are not saved on our servers.
  • Usage analytics: daily usage count, vibe preferences, and feature usage (stored locally on your device only using iOS UserDefaults).
  • Support communications: if you email us, we receive the information you include (e.g., your email address and message contents) to respond to your request.
  • Diagnostics (optional): Apple crash reports and diagnostics if you opt in via iOS settings. These are provided by Apple and may include device information and OS version. We do not receive your personal conversation data in crash reports.

No account required: The App does not require you to create an account with us, and we do not collect your name or other personal identifiers for App usage.

Subscription data: If you purchase a premium subscription ($3.99/week or $19.99 lifetime), payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive anonymous subscription validation receipts but no payment details.

AI Processing: When you generate responses, your input text is sent to OpenAI's servers for processing using their GPT-4o-mini model. This data is processed according to OpenAI's privacy policy and is not used to train their models.

Website — What We Collect

  • No cookies or analytics by us: The Website does not use tracking cookies or analytics tools.
  • Hosting and fonts: The Website is served via GitHub Pages and loads Google Fonts. Standard web server logs and font delivery logs may capture your IP address and browser information when pages and fonts are requested by your device.
  • Outbound links: When you click the App Store link, you leave our Website and are subject to Apple's policies.

How We Use Information

  • Provide App features: generate AI-powered dating responses, process conversation screenshots with OCR, and track usage limits locally on your device.
  • AI Response Generation: send your conversation text to OpenAI's servers to generate contextually appropriate responses based on your selected personality vibe.
  • Local storage: keep your App preferences and usage data stored locally on your device for optimal performance and privacy.
  • Support: respond to inquiries you send us.
  • Diagnostics: review Apple-provided crash and performance data to improve stability (if you opted in at the OS level).

Storage & Security

On your device

App data is stored locally within the App's iOS sandbox on your device and benefits from your device's security protections (such as passcode and Face ID/Touch ID) and iOS app sandboxing.

Local Device Storage

Your App preferences and usage data are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and Keychain Services for API keys. Conversation data is not permanently stored. We do not operate our own servers for your personal conversation data.

Third-Party AI Processing

When generating responses, your text input is temporarily sent to OpenAI's secure servers for processing. This data is not stored by us or used to train AI models. OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy.

Website

The Website is delivered over HTTPS. Hosting and font providers may process limited technical data (e.g., IP address) to serve the site and fonts.

Sharing

  • No sale of data: We do not sell your data.
  • Service providers: Apple (App Store, optional diagnostics), OpenAI (AI response generation), and hosting/font providers for the Website. These providers process data to deliver the App/Website functionality.
  • Legal: We may disclose information if required by law.

Your Choices

  • In-App: manage your API key and usage preferences in the App's settings; you can also uninstall the App to remove local App data from your device.
  • Local data: you can delete the App to remove all locally stored preferences and usage data, or clear data through iOS device storage settings.
  • Crash sharing: opt in or out of sharing diagnostics with developers in iOS settings.

GDPR/CCPA Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at spareisle@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

The App and Website are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided information, contact us to request deletion.

Data Retention

  • App preferences on device: retained until you delete the App or clear data through iOS storage settings.
  • Conversation data: not permanently stored by us. Temporarily processed by OpenAI for response generation and then discarded.
  • Support emails: retained as needed to address your request and for legal or operational requirements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post updates on this page and update the "Last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email spareisle@gmail.com.
